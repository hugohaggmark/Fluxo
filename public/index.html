Welcome to Fluxo

Follow these simple steps to get Kanban style statistics for your Trello Board.

To use Fluxo you need to connect To Trello here

Choose the board that you want to base your statistics on and then click the button "Next step".

In this step you mark the lists that make up your Kanban flow by clicking on them in the order you like. The chosen flow is shown in the area below as the lists are clicked.

The first list chosen will automatically count as the start of your process and the last list will be automatically count as the stop of your process. You can change that in the dropdown menus below.

When you click Next step you'll we be redirected to the your statistics.
